HOW IT WORKS

Upload Your Photo

Simply snap a photo of any ingredient list or nutrition label using your smartphone camera. Our advanced image recognition technology can read text from various angles and lighting conditions, making it easy to capture clear, readable images of product labels, recipe cards, or ingredient lists.

AI Analysis

Our cutting-edge artificial intelligence processes your uploaded image within seconds. Using advanced optical character recognition (OCR) and machine learning algorithms, we extract ingredient information and cross-reference it with our comprehensive nutritional database containing thousands of food items and their detailed nutritional profiles.

Get Results

Receive instant, detailed nutritional analysis including calories, macronutrients (proteins, carbs, fats), vitamins, minerals, and potential allergens. Our results are presented in easy-to-understand charts and graphs, helping you make informed dietary decisions and track your nutritional intake effectively.
