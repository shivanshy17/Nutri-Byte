About Us

NutriByte is dedicated to making nutrition information accessible to everyone. We believe that understanding what you eat shouldn't require a degree in nutrition science or hours of manual research.

Our team combines expertise in artificial intelligence, nutrition science, and user experience design to create tools that empower people to make healthier food choices. We leverage trusted data sources including the USDA FoodData Central and OpenFoodFacts API to ensure our nutritional information is accurate, comprehensive, and up-to-date.

Whether you're managing dietary restrictions, tracking fitness goals, or simply curious about your food's nutritional content, NutriByte provides reliable, instant analysis backed by official nutritional databases. Our AI technology cross-references ingredient information with these authoritative sources to deliver precise nutritional breakdowns you can trust.

We're committed to democratizing nutrition information, making it as easy as taking a photo to understand exactly what nutrients you're consuming. Our mission is to help people develop a better relationship with food through knowledge and transparency.
